Usage and Terms ` This specific protocol terminal refers to the processes and operation of Sentient Security Personnel (SSP) in regards to their workplace and behavior within reasonable operating conditions. The usage of this terminal is for both educational and referral purposes. Punishments given out by SSP should be based upon the data within this terminal. Note that these should be a baseline, and following to the letter may or may not be preffered. Case-by-case basis analysis is encouraged.
